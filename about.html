About

Our mission is to create kids' education for the creative age. We believe the future of education is children-centered and creativity-focused. We see education as an important way to help facilitate children's rich expressions. We value every aspect of children's uniqueness and appreciate their own stories above all.

The background of our team encompasses a variety of disciplines: pedagogy, arts, music, and engineering. Our educational approach is interdisciplinary and project-based, as are real world problems awaiting our children to solve. The world is changing rapidly. New technology becomes obsolete in a matter of years or even months. The only thing we could cultivate our children with is creative thinking and problem-solving ability. This is what defines us.
