Smiley Rhythm
Smiley Rhythm was started in 2015 as an Eurhythmics classroom for pre-k kids. Under Smiley Rhythm brand, there are Smiley Rhythm Music and Languages [f] and Smiley Rhythm Kindergarten [f] . Smiley Rhythm now offers a variety of courses ranging from languages, arts, and music. For more information, visit the official site .
Smiley Bits
Smiley Bits was launched in 2018 with a focus on computer science and technology education for children aged 5-12. Just like other artistic fields, we see computer science and technology as powerful tools for rich expression, through which kids can experience the magic of technology and combine it with their knowledge of arts to create something amazing and original.
